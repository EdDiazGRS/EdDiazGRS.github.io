Warrior I

Virabhadrasana I

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Type: Standing Pose
  • Duration: 30-60 seconds each side

Instructions

  1. Start in standing position
  2. Step one foot back 3-4 feet
  3. Bend front knee to 90 degrees
  4. Keep back foot at 45-degree angle
  5. Raise arms overhead
  6. Look forward or up at hands

Benefits

  • Strengthens legs and core
  • Opens hip flexors
  • Improves balance
  • Builds confidence
