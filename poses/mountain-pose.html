Mountain Pose

Tadasana

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Type: Standing Pose
  • Duration: 30-60 seconds

Instructions

  1. Stand with feet together or hip-width apart
  2. Ground down through all four corners of your feet
  3. Engage your thighs and draw your abdominals in
  4. Lengthen your spine and relax your shoulders
  5. Bring arms alongside your body
  6. Breathe deeply and maintain the pose

Benefits

  • Improves posture
  • Strengthens thighs, knees, and ankles
  • Increases body awareness
  • Creates a foundation for other standing poses
