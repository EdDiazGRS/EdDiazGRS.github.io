Child's Pose

Balasana

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Type: Resting Pose
  • Duration: 1-3 minutes

Instructions

  1. Kneel on the floor with toes together
  2. Sit back on your heels
  3. Separate your knees about hip-width apart
  4. Exhale and lay your torso between your thighs
  5. Extend your arms forward or alongside body
  6. Rest your forehead on the mat

Benefits

  • Releases back tension
  • Calms the mind
  • Relieves stress and fatigue
  • Gently stretches hips and thighs
