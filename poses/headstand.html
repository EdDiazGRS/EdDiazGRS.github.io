Headstand

Sirsasana

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Type: Inversion
  • Duration: Up to 3 minutes (with practice)

Instructions

  1. Create a stable base with forearms
  2. Interlace fingers, cup back of head
  3. Walk feet in closer to head
  4. Lift legs up together or one at a time
  5. Engage core and straighten legs
  6. Keep neck neutral, gaze straight ahead

Benefits

  • Builds upper body strength
  • Improves circulation
  • Enhances focus and concentration
  • Strengthens core and spine

Caution

Practice with a qualified instructor. Avoid if you have neck injuries, high blood pressure, or eye conditions.
