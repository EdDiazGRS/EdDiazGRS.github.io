Crow Pose

Bakasana

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Type: Arm Balance
  • Duration: 10-30 seconds

Instructions

  1. Start in a squat position
  2. Place hands shoulder-width apart
  3. Bend elbows slightly
  4. Rest knees high on back of arms
  5. Lean forward and lift feet
  6. Keep gaze slightly forward

Benefits

  • Builds arm and core strength
  • Improves balance
  • Increases focus
  • Builds confidence
