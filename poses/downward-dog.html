Downward Dog

Adho Mukha Svanasana

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Type: Inversion/Forward Bend
  • Duration: 5-8 breaths

Instructions

  1. Start on hands and knees
  2. Tuck your toes and lift your hips up and back
  3. Straighten your arms and press palms into mat
  4. Work on straightening legs and lowering heels
  5. Keep neck relaxed and gaze toward feet
  6. Form an inverted V-shape with your body

Benefits

  • Stretches hamstrings and calves
  • Strengthens arms and shoulders
  • Energizes the body
  • Improves circulation
