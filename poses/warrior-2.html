Warrior II

Virabhadrasana II

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Type: Standing Pose
  • Duration: 30-60 seconds each side

Instructions

  1. Start in a wide stance
  2. Turn right foot out 90 degrees
  3. Bend right knee over ankle
  4. Extend arms parallel to floor
  5. Gaze over front middle finger
  6. Keep torso centered and strong

Benefits

  • Strengthens legs and ankles
  • Opens hips and groin
  • Improves concentration
  • Builds stamina
