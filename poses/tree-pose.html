Tree Pose

Vrksasana

Basic Information

  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Type: Standing Balance Pose
  • Duration: 30-60 seconds each side

Instructions

  1. Start in Mountain Pose
  2. Shift weight to left foot
  3. Place right foot on inner left thigh or calf
  4. Avoid placing foot on knee
  5. Bring hands to heart or overhead
  6. Fix gaze on steady point

Benefits

  • Improves balance and focus
  • Strengthens ankles and calves
  • Opens hips
  • Builds concentration
